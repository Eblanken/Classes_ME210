Software Debugging Tips



Here are some common software writing tips and problems to keep in mind and look out for:
  • Did you call out the function/variable?
  • Are the pins properly identified as output or input in the code?
  • Does your variable need to be a global variable?
  • Are the conditions clearly defined for what state to go to next?
  • Keep the FSM simple first and debug problems one at a time. Write your FSM state by state as well. Start off with two states and make sure the robot can do those two states perfectly before moving on and adding the next state. This makes it so much easier to determine where the bug is coming from rather than just writing the whole FSM at once.
